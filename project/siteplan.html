The purpose of this site is an opportunity to collaborate with professionals from diverse industries who are looking to strengthen their online presence.

Color Schema for PROFBOOK

I selected this color schema for the main page, PROFBOOK will have a main page and two secondary pages (for the moment). The colors have been selected carefully, to give the appearance of professionalism, confidence and trustworthiness.

For the secondary pages I will use other colors, marketing page will use variations of red and black. And for the graphic design page, will use 3 or 5 colors.

Typography

The Typography selected for the webapge is "WORK SANS", this was taken from the Google Fonts page, I believe this typography will make not only the sitename look professional but the content and it will create a sense of confidence to the user.
